There is no especial Chicken Biryani among the South Asian dishes! It is a dish loaded with the aromatic spices and it is one of the most eminent dishes of the South Asian cuisine. Being at a Mama's Kitchen, we do not waiver on our emphasis that cooking our Special Chicken Biryani should have only the right measures of flavor and authenticity, making every bite a remarkable one. Dish beings from chicken after marinating in an alignment of remarkable spices with ginger, garlic, cumin, coriander, and garam masala as the initial ones. The chicken is then cooked with much care to perfection in order to give you a nice morsel at your table. With this we prepare a sturdy framing for our biryani. Rice is technologically the like 'nang doi'. The rice we do use with is told best basmati that is known with its long grains and pleasant smell. The rice is steamed until it is almost ready, after which it is layered with pieces of chicken. Also, the rice is seasoned with the aromatic spices like cardamoms, cinnamon, and cloves. It is like a symphony where you add different layers to the biryani to help the flavors to come together and to deepen as the biryani cooks. Our original Biryani recipe made with the "DUM" method where the pot is sealed and morsels of the dish is cooked over low temperature allows all flavors to come together.

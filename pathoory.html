Bhatoory Channay is one of Pakistani dishes that is very popular. It is the combination between fluffy deep-fried bread and a curry with chickpea. At Mama's Kitchen, we make the traditional combination with all the care in the world. Each ingredient in this dish is crafted perfectly to give the diner an unforgettable dining experience The "bhatoory" in Bhatoory Channay means bhature which is a deep fried bread made from a fermented dough. Our bhature is softer and fluffier, with a beautiful golden-brown exterior and a soft chewy inside. When fried, it rises beautifully becoming the perfect holder for some aromatic and rich channay. Channa, the second segment, offers a rich chickpea curry, which is loaded with spices and fragrances.
